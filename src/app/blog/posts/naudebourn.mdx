## Overview

NaudeBourn is a **luxury jewelry e-commerce platform** offering sustainably sourced 18k gold pieces. I developed a custom storefront powered by **Next.js** and integrated it with **Shopify Storefront APIs** to provide a fast, scalable shopping experience.

## Features

- **Next.js Frontend** – server-side rendering for SEO and fast navigation.  
- **Shopify Storefront API** – secure checkout and product management.  
- **Editor.js Blog** – rich-text content system for lifestyle and product blogs.  
- **SEO Optimizations** – structured metadata, robots.txt, sitemaps, GTM integration.  
- **Responsive UI** – mobile-first design with Tailwind CSS and Material UI.  

## Impact

- Increased page performance → ~30% faster load times.  
- Enhanced SEO → higher ranking on product keywords.  
- Delivered a **premium shopping experience** matching the brand identity.

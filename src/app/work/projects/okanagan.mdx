## Overview

Okanagan Gutter is a **local business website** designed to showcase gutter installation and maintenance services.  
I focused on performance, SEO, and analytics to help the client grow their digital presence.

## Features

- **Responsive UI** → Tailwind + Next.js for cross-device compatibility.  
- **Service showcase** with galleries and testimonials.  
- **SEO optimization** with structured data, metadata, and sitemaps.  
- **Google Tag Manager (GTM)** integration for traffic and conversion tracking.  

## Impact

- Improved local SEO rankings.  
- Increased lead conversion through a clear services funnel.
